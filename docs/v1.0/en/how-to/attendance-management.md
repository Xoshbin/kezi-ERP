# Attendance Management

The Attendance Management feature allows you to track employee clock-in and clock-out times, calculate total hours worked, and monitor attendance status.

## Key Features
- **Employee Selection:** Link attendance records to specific employees.
- **Clock In/Out:** Track precise times for employee shifts.
- **Attendance Date:** Record the specific day of attendance.
- **Status Tracking:** Monitor if an employee was present, absent, etc.
- **Total Hours:** Automatically or manually record the total hours worked.
