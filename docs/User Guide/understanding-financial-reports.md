# Understanding Financial Reports

Financial Reports provide insights into your business's financial health. Jmeryar offers a comprehensive suite of reports following standard accounting principles.

## Overview

The Reports Dashboard is your central hub for generating and viewing all financial reports. From here, you can access any report, set date ranges, and export data.

## Available Reports

### Financial Statements

Core reports that summarize your financial position:

*   **Profit and Loss (Income Statement)**: Shows revenue minus expenses for a period
*   **Balance Sheet**: Snapshot of assets, liabilities, and equity at a point in time
*   **Trial Balance**: List of all accounts with their debit and credit balances
*   **Cash Flow Statement**: Tracks cash movements by operating, investing, and financing activities

### Detailed Reports

Transaction-level detail for specific areas:

*   **General Ledger**: Complete transaction history for all accounts
*   **Partner Ledger**: Transactions and balances for customers or vendors

### Aging Reports

Track outstanding amounts over time:

*   **Aged Receivables**: Customer invoices grouped by how long they've been outstanding
*   **Aged Payables**: Vendor bills grouped by due date

### Tax Reports

Regulatory compliance reporting:

*   **Tax Report**: VAT/Sales tax collected and paid summary

### Consolidated Reports

For multi-company setups:

*   **Consolidated Profit and Loss**: Combined results across subsidiaries

## Generating Reports

1.  Navigate to **Accounting > Reports**.
2.  Select the desired report.
3.  Set parameters:
    *   **Date Range**: Start and end dates for the period
    *   **As Of Date**: For point-in-time reports like Balance Sheet
    *   **Comparison**: Optional comparison with previous periods
4.  Click **Generate Report**.

## Exporting Reports

All reports can be exported in multiple formats:
*   **PDF**: For printing and sharing
*   **Excel**: For further analysis
*   **CSV**: For data integration

## Best Practices

1.  **Monthly Review**: Generate P&L and Balance Sheet monthly
2.  **Reconcile First**: Ensure accounts are reconciled before running reports
3.  **Use Comparisons**: Compare to prior periods for trend analysis
4.  **Document Versions**: Save report versions for audit trail
